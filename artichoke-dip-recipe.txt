Sue Weaver's Artichoke Dip

1 Cup sour cream (can be light or non-fat)
1 Cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 Lg. pkg. Neufatchel or Cream cheese (8 oz?)
1 can of canned artichokes (drained and chopped)
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. chopped jalapenos ("tamed jalapenos" if you don't like heat - optional)
Sprinkle of paprika or smoked paprika

Mix together with a potato masher. Bake in a small casserole dish at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
